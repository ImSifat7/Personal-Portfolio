FAVORITE BUILD

Longform AI Explainers System

n8n · Airtable · OpenRouter AI Agent · Fish Audio · WaveSpeed AI · Nano Banana Pro · Gemini

My most advanced build — a complete longform AI explainer-video factory orchestrated through Airtable on scheduled triggers. An AI agent writes the full script, Fish Audio generates the voiceover, Nano Banana Pro creates B-roll images (analyzed by Gemini), and WaveSpeed AI renders talking-avatar and image-to-video clips — then logs every asset back to Airtable. Goes from idea to a finished, narrated explainer video completely hands-off.

Live in production · Demo on request

AI Motion Capture Studio

n8n · Replicate · fal.ai (WAN Animate) · OpenRouter · Telegram

An AI avatar & UGC video generator. From a driving video, a reference image, and a voice sample, it clones the voice, animates the image with the video's motion, and merges them into a finished clip — then auto-writes a social caption pack and delivers the video to Telegram, ready to post.

Live in production · Demo on request

Content Intelligence Engine

n8n · Apify · OpenRouter (LLM) · Google Sheets · Telegram

A multi-platform content & ad research system. From a single search, it scrapes top-performing content across 8 platforms — Meta Ads, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn, X, and Reddit — then runs the results through an AI layer that extracts the best-performing hooks, why they work, and fresh content ideas, and delivers a clean digest straight to Telegram. Turns hours of manual research into a one-click insight engine.

Live in production · Demo on request

AI Music Video Creator

n8n · GPT Agent · KIE / Suno · FFMPEG · YouTube

An end-to-end AI music & video factory. From a simple form prompt, an AI agent writes the song concept, generates an original AI track (Suno via KIE), pairs it with cover art, and renders a finished music video with FFMPEG — then auto-publishes to YouTube and logs everything to Sheets. Idea to published video, fully hands-off.

n8n · Webhook API · KIE (Veo 3.1) · Gmail

A webhook-powered AI video endpoint any app or frontend can call. It generates an AI image, then renders a full AI video with Google Veo 3.1 (via KIE), and emails the finished result automatically. A plug-and-play AI media backend for vibe-coded apps and client products.
